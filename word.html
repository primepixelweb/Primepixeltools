What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a tool that counts the number of words, characters, sentences and paragraphs in a piece of text. It is commonly used by writers, bloggers, students and editors to track writing length.

How to Use the Word Counter

1. Paste or type your text into the text box.
2. The tool automatically counts words and characters.
3. You can instantly see statistics like sentences and paragraphs.
