Convert text to uppercase, lowercase, title case or sentence case.

What is a Case Converter?

A case converter is a tool that converts text into different letter formats such as uppercase, lowercase, title case, and sentence case. It helps writers, students, and developers quickly format text.

How to Use the Case Converter

1. Enter or paste your text in the box.
2. Select the case format you want.
3. The tool instantly converts your text.
4. Copy the converted text.

Example

Input: hello world
Uppercase: HELLO WORLD
Title Case: Hello World

Frequently Asked Questions

What is uppercase?

Uppercase converts all letters to capital letters.

What is lowercase?

Lowercase converts all letters to small letters.

What is title case?

Title case capitalizes the first letter of each word.
