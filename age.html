What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is an online tool that calculates your exact age from your date of birth including years, months, days, hours, minutes and seconds.

How to Use the Age Calculator

1. Select your birth date.
2. Click calculate age.
3. The tool instantly shows your exact age.
4. Seconds update automatically every second.

Example

If your birth date is 1 January 2000 and today is 1 January 2026, your age will be 26 years.
