BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index instantly.

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a health indicator that measures body fat based on your height and weight. It helps determine whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How to Use the BMI Calculator

1. Enter your weight in kilograms.
2. Enter your height in centimeters.
3. Click calculate BMI.
4. The tool instantly shows your BMI and health category.

Example

If your weight is 70 kg and your height is 170 cm, your BMI will be approximately 24.2 which is considered normal weight.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a healthy BMI range?

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.

Is BMI accurate?

BMI is a general guideline and may not be fully accurate for athletes or bodybuilders.
